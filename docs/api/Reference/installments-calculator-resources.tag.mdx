When you want to provide your customer (that wants to pay for a purchase by a loan) an option to choose from available installment programs while he is still on your website, it is necessary to implement "installments calculator".

Choose one of the following approaches to integrate installments calculator into your website:

- A) Implementing your own installment calculator, with your FE in your design that uses designated API resources ([described below](#reference/installments-calculator-resources/implementing-own-installments-calculator)).

- B) Integrating Home Credit's javascript widget (described in [special chapter](#reference/javascript-widget-integration)) that:
  - provides GUI and front-end functionalities
  - process all needed calculations
  - provides necessary data to e-shop's back-end in order to easily use them within [*application* creation request](#reference/application-resources/create-application)

> NOTE: Implementation of installment calculator is optional. Customers can always use the installment calculator embedded in Home Credit's front-end.

## Implementing own installments calculator

In order to implement your own installment calculator (with your GUI design) you will need following resources:

- `/financing/v3/installmentProgramsOffers` - to find out available installment programs offers and their details

- `/financing/v3/installmentPrograms` - to find out details of a chosen installment program

- `/financing/v3/installmentProgramsDownPayments` - to find out down payment options within available installment programs offers
